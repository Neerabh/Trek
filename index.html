Himachal Pradesh
Dhankar
Monastery
A Spiritual Gem in the Heart of Spiti Valley
Dhankar Monastery, a spiritual oasis nestled amidst the rugged beauty of the Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh, India. Perched majestically atop a sheer cliff at an altitude of 3,894 meters (12,774 feet), Dhankar Monastery, also known as Dhankar Gompa, is a testament to the rich Buddhist heritage of the region.
Himachal Pradesh
Key
Monastery
A Spiritual Haven in the Himalayas
Perched atop a rugged hilltop in the Spiti Valley of Himachal Pradesh, India, Key Monastery stands as a beacon of tranquility and spiritual enlightenment. Dating back over a thousand years, this ancient Tibetan Buddhist monastery is renowned for its striking architecture, vibrant murals, and serene ambiance. Set against the backdrop of snow-capped peaks and azure skies.
